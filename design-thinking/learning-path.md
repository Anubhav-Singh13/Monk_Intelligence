# Learning Path — Design Thinking for Product Development

28 days. One page per day. 30–45 min of focused reading. The path below is the approved sequence — follow it in order.

---

## Module Overview

| Module | Days | Focus | Bottleneck addressed |
|--------|------|-------|---------------------|
| [01 — Foundations](./modules/01-foundations/overview.md) | 1–5 | Mental models, vocabulary, DT vs. Agile | Framing |
| [02 — Empathize](./modules/02-empathize/overview.md) | 6–10 | User research, observation, first synthesis artifacts | Observation technique |
| [03 — Define](./modules/03-define/overview.md) | 11–15 | Rest & Synthesize I, insight, POV, HMW, Synthesis Drill | **Synthesis (rate-limiting)** |
| [04 — Ideate](./modules/04-ideate/overview.md) | 16–18 | Brainstorming, ideation methods, convergence | Creative output quality |
| [05 — Prototype](./modules/05-prototype/overview.md) | 19–21 | What a prototype is, low-fi methods, what to build | Speed of learning |
| [06 — Test & Iterate](./modules/06-test-and-iterate/overview.md) | 22–25 | Rest & Synthesize II, user testing, feedback synthesis, iteration | Decision-making post-test |
| [07 — Integration & Capstone](./modules/07-integration-capstone/overview.md) | 26–28 | DT + Agile, mini-sprint planning, capstone project | Transfer to real work |

## The Rate-Limiting Bottleneck

**Synthesis** — moving from raw interview data to a sharp insight, a POV statement, and a HMW question — is the single sub-skill most likely to block learners at L1. It is addressed on:

- Day 9 (first encounter, empathy map)
- Day 12 (formally named and taught)
- Day 13 (applied to POV)
- Day 14 (applied to HMW)
- **Day 15 (dedicated drill — 7 progressive exercises)**
- Day 24 (reactivated in the Test phase)
- Day 28 (required for the capstone)

If any of these days feels difficult, that difficulty is the signal you have found the right bottleneck.
