# Module 04 — Ideate

**Days 16–18** · ~2 hours total

---

## What this module earns you

By the end of Day 18, you will be able to run a structured ideation session — moving from a HMW question to 20+ rough ideas, then converging on 2–3 concepts worth prototyping. More importantly, you will understand *why* standard brainstorming fails, and you will have two alternative methods that consistently unlock better ideas than round-the-table discussion.

This module is the shortest in the course (three days) because Ideate is the phase people most overestimate. It feels like the fun part — and it is — but creative output is not the bottleneck. The bottleneck is having a sharp enough HMW question (Module 03) to make Ideate productive, and having enough discipline in convergence (Day 18) to avoid drowning in ideas you can never test.
