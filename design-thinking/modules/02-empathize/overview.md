# Module 02 — Empathize

**Days 6–10** · ~3 hours total

---

## What this module earns you

By the end of Day 10, you will have a full empathy toolkit: two interview techniques, a field observation method, and two synthesis artifacts (empathy map and journey map). More importantly, you will understand *why* each tool exists — what cognitive gap it fills that the previous one misses.

This module is the most important in the course, because everything downstream — Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test — is only as good as the quality of what you observe here. A sharp insight is impossible without rich raw material. A sharp POV is impossible without a sharp insight. Most DT failures trace back to an Empathize phase that was too short, too shallow, or replaced by a survey.
