# Module 05 — Prototype

**Days 19–21** · ~2 hours total

---

## What this module earns you

By the end of Day 21, you will be able to build a prototype that is cheap enough to throw away and informative enough to generate learning — and you will have the judgment to decide *which* assumption to test, which is the skill that separates good prototypers from expensive ones.

This module reframes what a prototype is. Most product people think "prototype" means a Figma mock-up or a coded MVP. In DT, a prototype is anything tangible enough to put in front of a user and get a reaction — a paper sketch, a role-play, a storyboard, a cardboard object. The constraint is not fidelity. It is: *does it test the right assumption?*
