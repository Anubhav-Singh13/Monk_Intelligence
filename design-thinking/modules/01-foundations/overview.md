# Module 01 — Foundations

**Days 1–5** · ~2.5 hours total

---

## What this module earns you

By the end of Day 5, you will be able to explain — in plain language, without buzzwords — what Design Thinking is for, when it is the right tool, and how it sits alongside (not against) your Agile practice. You will have a navigational map of the full DT loop and a clear answer to the question most Agile practitioners ask first: *"Why do I need this if I'm already doing sprints?"*

This module installs mental models, not methods. No templates, no worksheets yet. The goal is that by Day 6, when you pick up the first empathy tool, you already understand what it is trying to do and why.
