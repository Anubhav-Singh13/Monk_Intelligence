# Module 07 — Integration & Capstone

**Days 26–28** · ~2.5 hours total

---

## What this module earns you

By the end of Day 28, you will have run a full DT cycle on a real problem from your own work — and you will have a set of artifacts (empathy notes, empathy map, POV statement, HMW question, concept sketch, paper prototype, test observations, and iteration decision) that prove you can do this without a script.

Days 26 and 27 are preparation: connecting DT to your Agile practice and building a runnable mini-sprint plan. Day 28 is the capstone — the transfer exercise that moves DT from a course topic to a professional skill.
